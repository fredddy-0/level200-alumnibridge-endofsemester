Welcome to Student Research Platform

INSIGHTHUB is a centralized digital platform designed for storing, managing, and exploring student research papers and capstone projects across different departments.

About INSIGHTHUB

The platform enables students to submit academic projects, supervisors to review them, and administrators to approve content before publication. It promotes collaboration, innovation, and academic visibility within the institution.

Key Features

  • Secure user login and registration
  • Project submission with files and links
  • Admin approval system before publication
  • Search and filter projects by department and year
  • Access to approved research papers
